Maakaf Community Mentoring Project

In our community, we aim to connect experienced programmers with those who are just starting their journey.

How does the connection between a beginner programmer and an experienced one happen?

To initiate a connection, approach the programmer through their calendar link provided in the table below. Your request should be precisely formulated - what do you want to gain from the conversation. General inquiries might not be received by the experienced programmers due to their valuable time.

How can an experienced programmer add their details to the project?

Simply edit the JSON file on which the mentors' table is based and add your details. The threshold condition for adding a mentor is a link to Calendly (or a similar alternative) that allows setting up a quarter-hour meeting with them.
